# Data Analytics for Business

Course taught in English to undergraduate and Master's-level students at EM Lyon Business School.

## Teaching volume

- 2024–2025: 2 groups, 24 hours per group
- 2025–2026: 5 groups, 24 hours per group
- Includes one Master's-level group

## Main topics

- Data analysis for business decision-making
- SQL
- Data visualisation
- Business-oriented data projects
- Interpretation and communication of analytical results

## Teaching activities

- Course delivery
- Practical exercises
- Project supervision
- Assessment and grading
